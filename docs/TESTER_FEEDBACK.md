# Tester Feedback

## Positive

* The interface is cleaner and scales well.
* Search narration makes routine gathering feel more atmospheric.
* Locations feel more distinct.
* Journal progression is becoming easier to understand.
* External testers continue to provide useful reports across versions.

## Suggestions

* Add more discoveries.
* Make discoveries unlock meaningful actions.
* Add more locations and landmarks.
* Add additional narrative variety.
* Give the player more reasons to revisit existing locations.

## Bugs Reported and Resolved

* Primitive Toolmaking did not populate in the Discoveries Journal.
* DiscoveryDatabase lacked direct discovery lookup.
* Track Animals action was not connected correctly.
* Completion scripts were called directly rather than instantiated.
* A discovery-journal function was incorrectly indented.
* Landmark event data failed to populate its linked landmark.
* Fresh Water could unlock while searching outside the River.

## Watch List

* Confirm Track Animals behavior in exported builds.
* Confirm discovery unlocks immediately rebuild available buttons.
* Continue testing older-save compatibility.
