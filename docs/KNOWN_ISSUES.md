# Known Issues

No known major issues.

## Minor

* Narrative phrases are currently stored directly in NarrativeGenerator.gd.
* Track Animals currently produces narrative and Knowledge but no physical tracking rewards.
* Action unlock requirements are currently hardcoded in GameManager.
* Discovery categories are not yet displayed.
* The Discoveries tab is always visible, even before the first discovery.
* Only one landmark and one world event currently exist.
* Expedition inventory weight and bulk limits are not yet enforced.
