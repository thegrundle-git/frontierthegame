v# 0.5.0-alpha3 — The World Remembers

Frontier gained persistent location history, first-visit narration, and its first permanent landmark.

The Journal now records visited locations and reveals a Landmarks tab when the player discovers the Abandoned Campsite.

This version established the foundation for environmental history, Finnley’s legacy, and future survivor-generated world memory.

## v0.5.1-alpha1 — The Age of Discovery

Frontier’s knowledge system began directly changing gameplay.

The Discoveries Journal became fully functional, and three new discoveries were added: Wild Flora, Fresh Water, and Animal Tracks.

Search actions gained generated atmospheric narration.

Animal Tracks became the first discovery to unlock a new world action, Track Animals, establishing knowledge as a pathway to new possibilities rather than merely a collection of Journal entries.
