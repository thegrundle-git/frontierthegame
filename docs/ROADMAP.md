# Frontier Roadmap

> "One survivor. One camp. One civilization."

This roadmap represents the planned direction of Frontier. Features may move, change, or be replaced as development continues.

---

# Current Version

## v0.1.8

Architecture Cleanup

Status: ✅ Complete

---

# Immediate Roadmap

## v0.1.9 — Biomes

Goal:

Introduce the first true locations.

Planned Features:

* Forest biome
* Current biome display
* BiomeData resources
* Biome-specific actions
* Biome-specific loot tables

Success Criteria:

Searching in different locations produces different resources.

---

## v0.2.0 — Campfire

Goal:

The player's first permanent structure.

Planned Features:

* Campfire recipe
* Build action
* Camp object
* Resting nearby
* Cook berries
* New discoveries related to fire

Success Criteria:

Players establish their first camp.

---

## v0.2.1 — Hunger

Goal:

Time becomes meaningful.

Planned Features:

* Hunger meter
* Food consumption
* Starvation penalties
* Eating actions
* Cooked food bonuses

Success Criteria:

Players must manage food while surviving.

---

## v0.3.0 — Settlement

Goal:

Expand beyond a single survivor.

Planned Features:

* Recruit survivors
* Assign jobs
* Shared civilization
* Shared storage
* Worker priorities

Success Criteria:

Multiple survivors work simultaneously.

---

## v0.4.0 — Exploration

Goal:

Create a larger world.

Planned Features:

* Multiple biomes
* Rivers
* Mountains
* Ruins
* Travel actions
* Random encounters

Success Criteria:

Players choose where to travel based on resources and risk.

---

## v0.5.0 — Technology

Goal:

Expand civilization discoveries.

Planned Features:

* Farming
* Pottery
* Carpentry
* Masonry
* Metalworking
* Animal Husbandry

Success Criteria:

The civilization evolves through discoveries rather than purchased upgrades.

---

## v0.6.0 — Economy

Goal:

Allow civilizations to trade.

Planned Features:

* Merchants
* Currency
* Trade routes
* Supply and demand
* Craft specialization

Success Criteria:

Players can sustain settlements through commerce.

---

## v0.7.0 — World Simulation

Goal:

The world changes without the player.

Planned Features:

* Seasons
* Weather
* Wildlife
* Resource regeneration
* NPC civilizations

Success Criteria:

The world feels alive.

---

## v1.0 — Frontier

Goal:

Complete the survival civilization experience.

The player begins alone in the wilderness.

Through discovery, craftsmanship, exploration, and leadership, they transform a small camp into a thriving civilization.

Every technology is earned.

Every building has a purpose.

Every discovery changes the way the world can be interacted with.

The frontier is conquered not through levels, but through understanding.
