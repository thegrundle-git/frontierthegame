# Frontier Design Pillars

## Player Agency

Players create their own stories through choices rather than scripted quests.

---

## Exploration First

Curiosity should be rewarded.

Players should always wonder:

"What is over there?"

---

## Knowledge Over Levels

Knowledge unlocks possibilities.

Learning is more important than grinding.

---

## Data-Driven Design

Content should be created primarily through resources rather than gameplay code.

---

## Persistent World

The world remembers.

Actions leave permanent marks.

---

## Survivor Legacy

Survivors eventually become history.

Civilizations outlive individuals.

---

## Meaningful Atmosphere

Small moments are more important than spectacle.
