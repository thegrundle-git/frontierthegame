# Frontier Lore

## The First Survivor

History remembers little with certainty.

Many stories begin with a single name.

Finnley.

Whether he was the first survivor or merely the first remembered is unknown.

His camps, journals, and discoveries remain scattered throughout the wilderness.

---

## The Wilderness

The world is ancient.

Nature slowly reclaims everything.

Civilizations rise and fall.

The land remembers.

---

## Legacy

Survivors eventually become history.

History becomes myth.

Myth shapes civilization.

---

## The Abandoned Campsite

A collapsed shelter stands beneath the trees beside a shallow ring of blackened stones.

Little remains of whoever stayed there.

The campsite currently bears no known name and provides no confirmed identity for its former occupant.

It may eventually become one of the earliest traces connected to the First Survivor.

---

##Finnley’s Legacy

Finnley’s identity should be revealed gradually.

The first camps, tools, and journal fragments associated with him should initially appear ordinary and anonymous.

Players should discover the connection themselves rather than being directly told.

Finnley remains:

the First Survivor;
a historical figure rather than an active character;
absent from the player’s present;
remembered imperfectly through physical traces and conflicting stories.
